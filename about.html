Theresa. Owner. Usually somewhere near the cat food.

One roof for the whole barnyard.

Animal Health Center started with a simple idea. The folks of Southwest Minnesota should not need three different stops for their dog's food, their cat's litter, and their cattle's vaccines. So we put it all under one roof. A full pet and livestock store, a pet resort for boarding and daycare, on-staff grooming, and a large animal veterinarian.

I started here years before I ever owned the place. In 2020 I bought the store I already loved, and not much changed, because it did not need to. We kept it local, friendly, and ready for animals big and small. We still know your dog by name. And if we do not have what you need, we will order it in.
