Pet Resort — Boarding

Your pet's vacation destination in Marshall, MN — dog & cat boarding with the care they deserve.

The Animal Health Center & Pet Resort is your pet's vacation destination! We offer boarding for both cats and dogs — with roomy kennels, family suites, and our cozy Kitty Korral. Our staff is dedicated to making sure your pets feel right at home while you're away.

Vaccination & Health Requirements

What your pet needs before arrival.

All animals must be current on their vaccinations and a prescription flea & tick preventative before boarding. Please bring records or have your vet send them ahead of your stay.

Dogs require

  • Rabies
  • Distemper
  • Parvo
  • Lepto
  • Bordetella (kennel cough)

Cats require

  • Rabies
  • Distemper
  • Feline leukemia (or negative test within 2 weeks)
Flea & tick: All pets must be current on prescription flea/tick medication. Accepted brands: Bravecto, Nexgard, Simparica, Credilio. Over-the-counter Frontline is also accepted — no other OTC brands. Questions? Call us at (507) 532-5000.
