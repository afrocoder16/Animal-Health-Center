Who we're shopping for

Stocked for every
animal you raise.

From the barn to the back door, our shelves are full of the name-brand vaccines, feed, and supplies your animals need. If we don't have it, we can probably get it.

Cattle

Vaccines from Merck, Zoetis, Elanco & Boehringer-Ingelheim, plus wormers, feed (Form-A-Feed, Purina), implants (Revalor, Synovex, Ralgro), VFD products, calf care, colostrum, and OB supplies.

Swine

Vaccines for PRRS, Circo, Mycoplasma, Erysipelas and more, plus supplements, disinfectants, marking supplies, and feed including Denagard (VFD) and Form-A-Feed.

Equine, Sheep & Goats

Vaccines, wormers, minerals, and general livestock supplies for horses, sheep, and goats — plus all the everyday essentials to keep your animals in top shape.

Pets

Premium dog & cat food (Fromm, Nutri-Source, Royal Canin, Purina Pro Plan, Diamond), plus treats, toys, beds, grooming supplies, flea & tick, and CBD products.
