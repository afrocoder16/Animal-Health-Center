# Images

Drop your photos into this folder using the exact filenames below. The site's
CSS ([../css/styles.css](../css/styles.css)) points at these local files, so as
long as the names match, your images appear automatically — no code changes needed.

JPG, PNG, or WebP all work; just keep the filename (including the extension)
matching what's listed here. Recommended: optimize/compress before adding so the
site stays fast.

| Filename | Where it shows | Suggested subject | Exact size (px) | Aspect ratio |
|----------|----------------|-------------------|-----------------|--------------|
| `hero.jpg` | Top hero banner (full width) | Storefront, or a warm pets + livestock shot | **1920 × 1080** | 16:9 landscape |
| `about-main.jpg` | About section — large photo | The store interior, owner, or a happy pet | **1200 × 1500** | 4:5 portrait |
| `about-sub.jpg` | About section — small overlapping photo | A dog/cat or a livestock close-up | **800 × 800** | 1:1 square |
| `departments.jpg` | "Who we're shopping for" section photo | Feed/supply aisle or livestock | **1000 × 1200** | 5:6 portrait |
| `team-1.jpg` | Team card 1 — Theresa Welu (Owner) | Headshot | **800 × 1067** | 3:4 portrait |
| `team-2.jpg` | Team card 2 — Dr. Scott Kuecker | Headshot | **800 × 1067** | 3:4 portrait |
| `team-3.jpg` | Team card 3 — Pet Resort Team | Headshot / staff with a dog | **800 × 1067** | 3:4 portrait |
| `team-4.jpg` | Team card 4 — Grooming Team | Headshot / groomer at work | **800 × 1067** | 3:4 portrait |
| `cta.jpg` | Bottom call-to-action band (full width) | A wide, friendly animal/store scene | **1920 × 1080** | 16:9 landscape |

> **Exact sizes** are the sweet spot — they're 2× the display size so photos stay crisp
> on high-resolution (retina) screens without being needlessly heavy. The **aspect ratio**
> is what matters most: match it and the image fills its frame perfectly with no awkward
> stretching or cropping. Bigger images than listed work too (the browser scales them
> down), but matching the exact size keeps the page fast.

> Tip: if you only want to replace some of them for now, that's fine — but any
> filename that's missing will show a blank/colored background until you add it.
