Cattle

One of our largest and most stocked departments. Whether you are running a cow-calf operation or finishing cattle, we carry what you need to keep your herd healthy and productive.

Vaccines

Merck, Zoetis, Elanco, Boehringer-Ingelheim. Merck refrigerated vaccines kept in stock. If you need a specific vaccine not on the shelf, ask us and we will do our best to get it.

Wormers

Feed grade, drench, pour-on, and injectable options available.

Feed

Form-A-Feed and Purina.

Supplements

Loose and block salt, Tracer, Rangeland, Form-A-Feed lick tubs.

Implants

Revalor, Synovex, Ralgro, Encore, Compudose.

Show Supplies

Sullivan Show Products for exhibition cattle.

VFD Products

CTC (10g, 20g, 50g), Tylan 10, Neo-Terra, and others available on request. We provide VFDs for our clients. If you are purchasing VFD products elsewhere, you must bring a valid VFD from your veterinarian.

General Cattle Supplies

Calf jackets, CIDRs, calf care products, colostrum replacers and supplements, calf milk replacer, OB and latex gloves, dehorning supplies, scour medications, and prescription medications.

Swine

From vaccines to disinfectants to VFD feed products, we carry what pork producers in Southwest Minnesota rely on.

Vaccines

PRRS, Ileitis, Salmonella, Erysipelas, Circo, Mycoplasma, Parvo.

Supplements

Form-A-Feed, Bluelite, Techmix products.

Disinfectants

Intervention, Synergize, Barn Storm, Tek-Trol.

Marking Supplies

Paint sticks, twist paint sticks, aerosol, Sprayolo.

Feed

Denagard (VFD), Form-A-Feed, T.E.N. App-A-Tite.

Equine

From performance horse nutrition to everyday tack and hoof care, our equine department covers the full range of what horse owners in the area need.

Vaccinations

Vetera Gold, Vetera EWT plus WNV, Pneumabort, and others available by request.

Supplements

Mineral blocks, EquiLix, EquiPride, various fat supplements, Ralco Slick Mix.

Feed

Purina, ProElite by Progressive, Triple Crown, oats, beet pulp.

Hay Chix Hay Nets

Round bale nets, small square nets, half bale nets, and replacement nets.

Tack

Halters, bridle sets, saddle pads, lead ropes, snaps, cinches, shoo fly boots, fly masks.

General Equine Supplies

Hoof care products, rasps, horse shoes, vet wrap, buckets, shampoos, fly control, wound care, forks.

Sheep and Goats

Whether you are raising sheep for market or keeping goats on the property, we carry the essentials for lamb and kid care.

What we carry

Milk replacer, vaccines, prescription antibiotics, colostrum, and bottle feeding supplies including tube feeders, bottles, and nipples.

Need something not on this list? Call us at (507) 532-5000 and we will do our best to source it for you.

General Livestock

The essentials every operation needs regardless of what you raise. We keep a large and consistent inventory of syringes, needles, disinfectants, marking supplies, wound care, bedding, and more.

Syringes and Needles

Plastic syringes from 1cc to 60cc. Automatic syringes from AllFlex, Hauptner, PrimaTech, and Ideal, including replacement parts. Needles from 14g to 20g, aluminum hub. Detectable needles also in stock.

Protective Gear

Disposable boots (Treader and Agri-Pro), disposable coveralls full suit with zipper in sizes M to 3XL, disposable gloves in nitrile and latex sizes S to XXL.

Marking Supplies

Paint sticks, aerosols, and concentrated marking paints.

Prods and Sort Sticks

HotShot, SharpShock, Magrath, rattle paddles, Kane paddles, Kane rattle cans, sorting boards.

Pest Control

Motomco bait and bait stations.

Disinfectants

Rescue, Intervention, Tektrol, Synergize, Chlorhexidine, BarnStorm, FreshStart, Virkon S.

Feed and Bagged Supplies

Yaggie's oats, beet pulp, salt blocks and bags, sodium bicarb, copper sulfate, barn lime.

Bedding

Marth bedding pellets and wood shavings.

Clippers and Blades

Available in store.

Wound Care

Vetericyn, Banixx, vet wrap, bandages, gauze, scalpels, umbilical tape and needles, DMSO, NFZ Puffer, iodine, Triodine.

Other

Thermometers and lubricant.

Veterinary

Large animal veterinary support on site

Dr. Scott Kuecker has been serving large animal clients in Southwest Minnesota since 1992. A University of Minnesota graduate, Dr. Kuecker brings decades of experience with cattle, swine, equine, sheep, and goats. He is available at Animal Health Center and works closely with the team at the Marshall Animal Clinic to serve the full range of large animal veterinary needs in the region.
