Medical & Injection Supplies

Syringes & Needles

Plastic syringes 1cc–60cc. Automatic syringes: AllFlex, Hauptner, PrimaTech, Ideal — plus replacement parts. Needles 14g–20g aluminum hub; detectable needles available.

Animal Handling

Prods & sort sticks — HotShot, SharpShock, Magrath, rattle paddles, Kane paddles, Kane rattle cans, sorting boards.

Marking & ID

Marking paints in sticks, aerosol, and concentrated formula. Marking supplies for swine — twist paint sticks, Sprayolo.

Sanitation & Disinfectants
