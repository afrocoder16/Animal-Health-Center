Signature programs

Built for cow-calf,
stocker & feedlot.

Choose a managed program and we handle the calendar, the protocols, and the paperwork — so nothing slips between seasons.

Cow-Calf Wellness

Pre-breeding exams, pregnancy diagnosis, calving support, and a vaccine schedule mapped to your calving window. Includes mineral and BCS monitoring.

Stocker & Backgrounding

Arrival processing, respiratory disease prevention, implant strategy, and gain monitoring designed to lower morbidity and protect average daily gain.

Feedlot Health Management

Chute-side protocols, pen-rider training, treatment records, and weekly consults that keep death loss low and performance high across the feeding period.

Show & Seedstock Care

Premium individualized care for registered and show cattle — soundness, reproduction, and health certification for sales and transport.

How we work

A partnership in four steps.

Clear, repeatable, and built around your calendar — no guesswork.

  1. 01

    Walk the operation

    We start on-site — assessing facilities, records, nutrition, and goals to understand your reality.

  2. 02

    Build the protocol

    A written, season-by-season health plan with vaccines, diagnostics, and benchmarks you can measure.

  3. 03

    Execute & monitor

    Scheduled visits plus on-call support, with every treatment logged in your digital herd record.

  4. 04

    Review & refine

    Quarterly reviews translate the data into the next season's plan — always improving the numbers.
