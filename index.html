Animal Health Center has been Marshall's go-to for animals big and small since 1998. From pet food and grooming to livestock vaccines and supplies, we're the friendly, family-run shop that keeps Southwest Minnesota's animals healthy and happy.

  • Full retail store for pets & livestock alike
  • Pet Resort dog & cat boarding and doggy daycare
  • On-staff grooming: bathing, brushing & nails
  • We work closely with the vets at Marshall Animal Clinic
